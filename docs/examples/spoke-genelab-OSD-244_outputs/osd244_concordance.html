Concordance of differentially expressed genes

OSD-244 · Rodent Research-6 · Space Flight vs Ground Control · ~30 day vs ~60 day timepoints

Each point is one of 322 genes that was significantly differentially expressed (adj. p ≤ 0.05) in both the ~30 day and ~60 day SF vs GC comparisons. The dashed gray diagonal is y = x (perfect concordance). Pearson r = 0.80 across the 322 shared genes.
