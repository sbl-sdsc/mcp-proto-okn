## Common Contaminants at Superfund Sites

The knowledge graph contains detailed information about numerous Superfund sites across the United States, identified by ZIP codes. These sites contain a wide range of hazardous contaminants in various environmental media.

### Major Categories of Contaminants:

**Heavy Metals:**
- Arsenic - Found in groundwater, soil, sediment, and leachate at multiple sites
- Lead - Present in soil, groundwater, surface water, sediment, and air emissions
- Mercury - Detected in groundwater, soil, sediment, surface water, leachate, and solid waste
- Chromium - Found in soil, groundwater, sediment, and air emissions
- Cadmium - Present in soil, groundwater, and sediment
- Copper, Nickel, Zinc, Barium, Aluminum, Antimony, Beryllium, Selenium, Thallium

**Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs):**
- Benzene - Found in soil, groundwater, air, landfill gas
- Trichloroethylene - In soil, groundwater, sediment, landfill gas
- Tetrachloroethylene - In groundwater and soil
- Vinyl Chloride - In soil, groundwater, air, sediment, landfill gas
- Ethylbenzene - In soil, groundwater, air emissions
- 1,2-Dichloroethylene - In groundwater, soil, sediment
- Chloroform, Methylene Chloride, Carbon Tetrachloride
- Various chlorinated solvents (1,1,1-Trichloroethane, 1,1,2-Trichloroethane, etc.)

**Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbons (PAHs):**
- Benzo[a]pyrene - In soil and sediment
- Benz(a)anthracene - In soil, sediment, groundwater
- Benzo[b]fluoranthene - In soil, sediment, groundwater
- Chrysene, Naphthalene, Anthracene, Acenaphthene

**Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs):**
- Various PCB congeners including hexachlorobiphenyls and pentachlorobiphenyls in soil and groundwater

**Other Organic Compounds:**
- Bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate
- Dioxins (2,3,7,8-Tetrachlorodibenzo-P-dioxin)
- Chlorobenzenes (1,2-Dichlorobenzene, 1,4-Dichlorobenzene, etc.)
- Pesticides (Dieldrin, Endosulfan, Chlordane)
