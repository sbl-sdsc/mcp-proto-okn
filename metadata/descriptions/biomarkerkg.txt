The Biomarker Knowledge Graph (BiomarkerKG) is a biomedical RDF knowledge graph derived from the BiomarkerKB/Clinical Biomarkers resource that organizes clinically relevant biomarkers and their relationships to diseases, biological entities, and anatomical sources. Its schema is grounded in the Ontology for Biomarkers of Clinical Interest (OBCI). Each biomarker is linked to a disease or condition through relationships that capture its clinical role, following the BEST classification system: diagnostic for, prognostic for, monitors status of, and indicates risk of developing. Biomarkers are further connected to their assessed entities, such as genes and chemical compounds that are present or that show above-normal or below-normal levels, and to the anatomical structures from which measurement samples are taken. Diseases use Disease Ontology and OMIM identifiers, genes and compounds use PubChem and NCBI Gene identifiers, and anatomy uses UBERON. The graph supports questions about which biomarkers are diagnostic, prognostic, or risk indicators for specific diseases, which molecular entities underlie them, and what tissues are sampled to measure them.
